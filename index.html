Hortus Forum

We are Cornell's Undergraduate Horticulture Club. We spread our passion for plants and make horticulture more accessible!

Hortus Forum aims to cultivate a positive social community which fosters a passion for plants and teaches the value of horticulture. We provide educational experiences beyond the classroom, and offer diverse avenues for exploring horticulture, spanning various plant-based activities and experiences. Through these initiatives, Hortus Forum spreads the knowledge of horticulture and our passion for plants at Cornell.
